# Post-Study Interview Guide

Target time: 5 minutes per participant.

## Opening

Thank you for completing the study. I am going to ask a few short questions about your experience with the two audio versions. There are no right or wrong answers.

## Questions

1. Which audio condition was easier to understand, and why?
2. Were there any expressions where the semantic explanation helped you?
3. Were there any expressions where the semantic explanation felt too long or distracting?
4. What types of mathematical symbols were hardest to follow by audio?
5. If this tool were used in a real class or study session, what would you change?

## Coding Categories

Use these initial codes when reviewing transcripts:

- semantic_clarity
- notation_confusion
- pacing_or_pause
- cognitive_load
- verbosity
- domain_familiarity
- preference_notation_only
- preference_semantic
- suggested_improvement
